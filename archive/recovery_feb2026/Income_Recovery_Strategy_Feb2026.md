## IMPORTANT RISK DISCLAIMERS

1. **Options can expire worthless** - You can lose 100% of premium paid
2. **Assignment risk** - Cash-secured puts may force you to buy shares at a loss
3. **IV crush** - Selling options before earnings can be profitable but risky
4. **Small account limitations** - Cannot diversify as much; single bad trade can wipe out months of gains
5. **YieldMax ETFs** - Distributions are mostly return of capital, NAV erodes; not true income
6. **MSTR/RIOT** - Crypto-correlated, can drop 30-50% in a month
7. **NIO** - Chinese regulatory risk, potential delisting risk
8. All prices as of February 19, 2026 and subject to change
